Our Privacy Commitment

Mini Cycle is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe in transparency and that your task data should stay with you. We believe that you should always be in control of how your data is being used. This policy explains exactly how we handle your information - spoiler alert: we barely handle any at all.

The Simple Truth

We don't collect, store, or have access to your personal data. Period.

Mini Cycle runs entirely in your web browser using local storage technology. Your tasks, cycles, settings, and all other data never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

What Data Mini Cycle Uses

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Task Data: Your task lists, cycle configurations, and completion history
  • App Settings: Your preferences for themes, notifications, and interface options
  • Usage Analytics: Local statistics like cycle completion counts and streaks (never transmitted)
  • Recurring Templates: Your saved recurring task configurations

How Local Storage Works

  • All data is stored using your browser's localStorage technology
  • Data remains on your specific device and browser
  • We cannot access this data remotely
  • Data persists until you clear it or uninstall your browser

  • ‚ùå Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • ‚ùå Usage analytics or tracking data
  • ‚ùå Device information or IP addresses
  • ‚ùå Location data
  • ‚ùå Cookies for tracking purposes
  • ‚ùå Task content or personal information

Data You Control

Export/Backup

  • You can export your data as .mcyc files at any time
  • Export files contain only your task data - no tracking information
  • You control where these files are stored and who has access

Data Deletion

  • Clear your data anytime through the app's "Factory Reset" option
  • Delete individual tasks or cycles as needed
  • Clear your browser data to remove all Mini Cycle information

Third-Party Services

Mini Cycle does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app runs completely independently.

Technical Safeguards

Data Security

  • Your data never transmits over the internet
  • No remote servers store your information
  • Input sanitization protects against malicious code injection
  • Local data is stored in standard browser security contexts

Browser Compatibility

  • Works with modern browsers that support localStorage
  • Graceful degradation when storage is unavailable
  • No external dependencies that could compromise privacy

Changes to This Policy

If we ever need to update this privacy policy (for example, if we add optional cloud features), we will:

  • Notify users prominently in the app
  • Maintain the same privacy-first approach
  • Give users full control over any new data handling

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing for us to:

  • Delete (it's already local to you)
  • Correct (you control it directly)
  • Transfer (you can export it anytime)
  • Access (we can't see it anyway)

Developer Transparency

Mini Cycle is developed by an independent developer who believes in privacy-focused software. The app's client-side architecture was specifically chosen to ensure user privacy and data ownership.

Legal Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws

Since we don't collect personal data, most privacy regulations don't apply to our data practices, but we've designed Mini Cycle to exceed privacy standards anyway.

Bottom Line

Mini Cycle is designed so that your personal task data stays exactly where it belongs - with you. We can't see your tasks, we prioritize privacy and transparency. If there were to be any future changes, they would be communicated clearly and always with your consent.
